What is VSA?

The Vietnamese Student Association strives to promote Vietnamese culture throughout the UNC Charlotte campus and the Charlotte community. We encourage the understanding and learning of the Vietnamese culture through our events.

When do we have our general body meeting?

The Vietnamese Student Association has been consistenly holding general body meetings every other Thursday such as the Tet/Lunar New Year to educate about the Vietnamese holidays and embrace our culture which allows the students of UNC Charlotte to learn more about our culture.
